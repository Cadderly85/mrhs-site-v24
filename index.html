Our Approach

Four Pillars of Excellence

Our comprehensive approach to pain management integrates four foundational disciplines to deliver exceptional patient outcomes.

01

Comprehensive Pain Management

We begin with a thorough evaluation of each patient's condition, medical history, and lifestyle factors. Our multidisciplinary team works together to develop individualized treatment plans that address the full spectrum of pain — from acute injury to chronic conditions — ensuring no aspect of care is overlooked.

02

Advanced Interventional Treatments

Utilizing the latest minimally invasive techniques and image-guided procedures, our interventional specialists target pain at its source. These precision treatments offer patients faster recovery times, reduced risk, and superior outcomes compared to traditional surgical approaches.

03

Regenerative Medicine

Our regenerative therapies harness the body's natural healing mechanisms to repair damaged tissues and restore function. Through advanced biologic treatments and cutting-edge cellular therapies, we help patients achieve lasting relief and improved quality of life.

04

Integrative Wellness Therapies

True healing extends beyond procedures. We incorporate complementary wellness strategies — including nutritional guidance, exercise protocols, and mind-body practices — to support whole-body recovery and empower patients to maintain lasting results.

Research

Driven to Innovate &
Produce Leading Clinical Research

Our foundational work with Harvard Medical School and other leading institutions demonstrates our deep expertise and a commitment to producing leading academic research.

We strive to advance the study and practice of Restorative Health Sciences and the promotion of universal access to an improved quality of life.

PainQx Platform

An AI-powered cloud-based solution using EEG data to classify a patient's pain state at the point of care. This innovative technology supports chronic pain assessment in populations that cannot accurately articulate their pain, such as Alzheimer's patients.

Ketamine Virtual Reality (KVR)

Use of VR video and audio stimulation to enhance ketamine therapy for chronic pain and depression. Dr. Canty pioneered this innovative treatment approach. The first research analysis has been conducted, showing promising results for patients.

Novel Therapy for Wellbeing

Research measuring the effects of a novel application of accepted intervention combined with behavioral therapy for immediate effect on sense of wellbeing and the ability to resist intrusive thoughts.
